"For the Neo4j Partner Prize, we didn't just want to use a graph database as a normal data store. We used Neo4j to map out the complex web of team relationships, task assignments, and workloads.

Our backend runs Cypher queries to extract 'Task Chains' and workload distributions. We then feed that graph data directly into our AI engine powered by Groq. As you can see on the Leader Dashboard, the AI uses the graph to automatically detect hidden bottlenecks—like if a specific member is overloaded and blocking multiple dependent tasks. This gives leaders instant, graph-powered intelligence that a flat database could never provide."
